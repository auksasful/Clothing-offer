My Journey with Halara

After noticing my TikTok For You Page flooded with try-ons and haul videos from activewear brand Halara, I couldn’t resist giving it a try myself. Instead of blindly clicking an ad, I visited their website and was impressed by the wide range of styles, inclusive sizing, and wallet-friendly prices. With a bit of research backing up my decision, I dove into testing the brand firsthand—and I wasn’t disappointed.

I even rallied a few fellow editors to wear Halara’s most popular pieces during our workdays, workouts, and casual outings. The versatility of these designs—from a supportive workout dress to a trendy skirt with built-in shorts—made it clear that Halara truly is an exciting activewear option.

Overall Impressions

I was initially skeptical about Halara’s affordability, but every piece exceeded my expectations. The fabrics are incredibly soft and stretchy—and even after multiple washes, they hold up beautifully without pilling.

Sizing is impressively inclusive (ranging from XS to 3X) and most items fit true to size. While a couple of designs (like the built-in shorts on the Easy Peezy Dress) might not be perfect for high-intensity workouts, everyday wear and light cardio are a breeze.

What truly sets Halara apart is its commitment to design innovation. Inspired by real customer feedback (yes—one clever idea was the bathroom-friendly shorts design), the brand consistently delivers on both style and functionality.

The verdict? Halara is a must-try for anyone looking to revamp their wardrobe with activewear that easily transitions from workout sessions to social outings—all at a very affordable price.
